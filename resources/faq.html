Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Fyndo's AI-powered workplace intelligence platform

What is Fyndo and how does it work?

+

Fyndo is an AI-powered workplace intelligence platform that helps teams find, organize, and leverage their institutional knowledge. It connects to your existing tools (Slack, Gmail, Drive, etc.) and uses advanced AI to provide instant answers, intelligent search, and automated insights across your entire digital workplace.

The platform works by:

  • Integrating with your existing tools and data sources
  • Analyzing and indexing your content using AI
  • Providing intelligent search and question-answering capabilities
  • Offering automated insights and recommendations

How long does it take to set up Fyndo?

+

Most teams can get started with Fyndo in under 30 minutes. The setup process includes:

  • Initial setup: 5-10 minutes to create your account and configure basic settings
  • Integrations: 10-15 minutes to connect your primary tools (Slack, Google Workspace, etc.)
  • Data indexing: 1-24 hours for AI to process and index your content (happens in background)
  • Team onboarding: 5-10 minutes per team member

You can start seeing value immediately, with full capabilities available once indexing is complete.

Do I need technical expertise to use Fyndo?

+

No technical expertise is required. Fyndo is designed to be user-friendly for all team members:

  • No-code setup: Point-and-click integration with popular tools
  • Intuitive interface: Simple search and chat-based interactions
  • Guided onboarding: Step-by-step tutorials and help
  • 24/7 support: Our team is available to help with any questions

What makes Fyndo different from other search tools?

+

Fyndo goes beyond traditional search with AI-powered intelligence:

  • Contextual understanding: Understands intent, not just keywords
  • Cross-platform search: Search all your tools from one place
  • Intelligent answers: Provides direct answers, not just links
  • Proactive insights: Surfaces relevant information you didn't know to look for
  • Continuous learning: Gets smarter with your team's usage patterns

Can Fyndo understand context and provide personalized results?

+

Yes, Fyndo's AI engine provides highly personalized and contextual results:

  • Role-based results: Prioritizes information relevant to your job function
  • Project context: Understands what projects you're working on
  • Team dynamics: Knows who you collaborate with most
  • Historical patterns: Learns from your search and interaction history
  • Real-time context: Considers your current activities and calendar

What types of content can Fyndo search and analyze?

+

Fyndo can process and search virtually any type of business content:

  • Documents: PDFs, Word docs, spreadsheets, presentations
  • Communications: Emails, Slack messages, Teams chats
  • Code: Repositories, documentation, comments
  • Media: Images, videos, audio files (with transcription)
  • Structured data: CRM records, project management tools
  • Web content: Internal wikis, websites, knowledge bases

How secure is my data with Fyndo?

+

Security is our top priority. Fyndo implements enterprise-grade security measures:

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption in transit and at rest
  • Access control: Role-based permissions and SSO integration
  • Infrastructure: SOC 2 compliant hosting on AWS/Azure
  • Privacy: Your data is never used to train AI models
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and incident response
  • Compliance: GDPR, CCPA, and industry-specific compliance

How do integrations work? Do you store all my data?

+

Fyndo uses a smart approach to data handling:

  • Selective indexing: We only index metadata and key content, not full files
  • Real-time access: Most content stays in your original tools
  • Encrypted storage: Any stored data is fully encrypted
  • Automatic sync: Changes in your tools are reflected immediately
  • Data minimization: We only access what's needed for search and AI features
